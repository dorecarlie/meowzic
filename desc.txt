Meowzic is a webapplication that has users create customizable playlists based on songs from artists with punny cat names. 
For example, lady gaga would be lady paw paw and katy perry would be katy purry. 
This app's goal is to make organzing your song playlists easily for cats to enjoy the sound of music.
The intended user would be for cats, cat owners, or people fond of cats.
With this app, users can create their own personal cat account with a username, icon and password and login to their personalized music library.
Once logged in, users will click a '+' sign to create a playlist, and they can add songs through a catalog onto that playlist.
The users can also edit the playlist name and icon, and everytime a new song is added, the playlist will update.

Future implemations: users can have joint playlists which eachother and add friends through the app
